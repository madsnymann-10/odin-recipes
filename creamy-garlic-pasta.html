Garlic Pasta

A great Garlicy pasta dish in under 30 minutes.

A dish i personally use, when im in hurry or the budget is low. Enjoy this simple yet great meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package dry penne pasta
  • Â½ cup olive oil
  • 1 medium head garlic, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, add oil and garlic to a large skillet over low heat. Cook until oil is hot and garlic is sizzling, 8 to 12 minutes. Remove from the heat and stir in parsley, basil, oregano, and pepper flakes.
  3. Drain pasta and transfer to a large bowl. Add oil and garlic mixture, toss to coat, and let sit for 3 to 5 minutes. Sprinkle with Parmesan and serve.
